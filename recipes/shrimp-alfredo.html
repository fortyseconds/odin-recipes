Shrimp Fettuccine Alfredo

Delicious fettuccine alfredo, complete with shrimp

This recipe combines the classic fettucine alfredo with shrimp and parsley to add a flavorful touch.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fettucine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 pound cooked shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup half and half
  • 6 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt to taste
